
📱 WHATSAPP MESSAGING GUIDE - PESTCONTROL99

This document explains how to use the manual WhatsApp messaging system integrated into the CRM. Use these buttons to communicate quickly with clients and technicians using professional templates.

--------------------------------------------------
1. MESSAGE TEMPLATES OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------

Template | Recipient | Purpose
--------------------------------------------------
T1: Booking Confirmation | Customer | To confirm a new service booking with ID and details.
T2: Technician Assigned | Customer | To notify the client of the technician's name and contact.
T3: Service Rates / Inquiry | Customer | To send full price charts and service plans to new leads.
T4: Job Details | Technician | To send full service details (Address, Notes, etc.) to the staff.

--------------------------------------------------
2. WHERE TO FIND THE BUTTONS
--------------------------------------------------

A. VIEW ENQUIRIES SCREEN (NEW LEADS)
Used for sending Service Rates (T3) to potential customers.
- Location: Look at the "Customer Info" column.
- Button: Small green WhatsApp icon next to the mobile number.
- Usage: Click this immediately after a client makes an inquiry to send them our price list.

B. JOB CARDS DASHBOARD (ACTIVE BOOKINGS)
Icons are located in the "Actions" column (Last column on the right).
- Green Icon: Sends Booking Confirmation (T1) to the client.
- Blue Icon: Sends Job Details (T4) to the technician's WhatsApp.
- Indigo Icon: Sends Technician Assigned (T2) info to the client.

NOTE: The Blue and Indigo icons only appear AFTER a technician has been assigned to the job.

C. EDIT BOOKING PAGE
Located at the top-right header of the edit screen.
- Allows you to send messages while you are updating a booking.
- Feature: If you change the time or address on the page, the WhatsApp buttons will send the updated details even before you click the "Update" button.

--------------------------------------------------
3. ICON REFERENCE & COLOR CODING
--------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------

5. INSTRUCTIONS FOR TEAM
--------------------------------------------------
1. Ensure you have WhatsApp Web logged in or the WhatsApp Desktop App installed.
2. Click the appropriate icon based on the stage of the booking.
3. A new tab will open with the pre-filled message.
4. Review the message and press SEND in WhatsApp.
